Many News 9

June 2021, website v2.3.8

It's been a productive period, both on our website and in the Many Isles as a whole.

Warning: Trade Agreement Update

We've updated the Trader's Agreement to suit our new digital store and general situation. The changes include a removal of product requirements for free goodies so you can publish them in whatever format you want, a rule structure for handling the new creator support feature, and an obligation for the Pantheon to warn all traders when the Agreement is changed. See you in the digital library!

This Den Shall Be Our Tomb

Our first meddling in "base fortification and defense" adventures. As a tribe of kobolds, fortify and defend your den against mean human hunters who try to weed you out! Find it in the digital library. By the way, there's tons of new products out by various creators!

Population Tool

A project that was recently completed is an awesome population model. Check it out!

Warning: Imminent Pricing Changes

We'll be revisiting parts of the digital store soon, and this will include a pricing increase for tiers, which grant eternal access to cool modules such as the Handbook of Dark Secrets or Mildred's Guide to Hags. So if you're looking to get a tier, make sure you purchase it soon!

Project Status: Rise of Humankind

We've gotten far in the Rise of Humankind project. The Pantheon has almost completed editing the first draft, at which point it will be handed over to the community for feedback! If you're interested in helping out by reading a chapter or two, you can voice it through whichever channel to the Patheon, notably on discord. View the project in the wiki.
