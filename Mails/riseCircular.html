Out of the light of the stars, under the gaze of a new moon, a new race shall be born. Sixty-six of the plain children of the Silmarilus shall stand, proud and gusted by the wind, in a circle by the Standing Stones, receiving the greatest birth of all.
Nature shall once more create, and make from children children more. The sons and daughters of nature's stars they shall be, each and every one a purveyor of its grand designs.

The Moon's Prophecy, 13'467 b. OW

The Project

The Pantheon's current main project is the Rise of Humankind, a print history book of the first half of humanity's history in the Many Isles. You can check out the project, and the current state of its draft, on its wiki page.

Help us Out!

We just completed the book's 1st Draft and are looking for beta readers to give feedback. Are you interested in reading a subchapter and telling us what you think so we can make it better? Join the project on discord, or contact us by mail!

Weave a Tale

The Many Isles is an open community setting. If you've got a cool fantasy story, or have an inspiration that you'd love to see included in the book, feel free to share! And remember, you can always get feedback on your own worldbuilding on discord or on our subreddit!
