Welcome to the Create Page, Adventurer!

This page is devoted to our supreme goal, creation. As you know, we (the Elder Gods) are fanatics of creating stuff. That's why the Many Isles exist. In Create!, you can help us in this heroic enterprise.

The channels on the left side show the different ways in which we -together- can continue crafting things. If you were to create an account in the Many Isles - which we'd greatly appreciate - you'd get awesome pre-release information, general Many Isle news, and some special goodies. Combine those by connecting your Many Isles account to your Patreon tier, and you're good to go!

Contact Us

As you know, we love creating. And it's great to create what others want as well! For that reason, and out of general helpfulness, feel free to write us.
You can easily reach us via our email, godsofmanyisles@gmail.com. We're open for any new suggestions, be it in our content, for new products, for pointing out mistakes, or even questions of any kind. We're glad to help.
Soon, we will have created features such as direct messaging within this page. Check back to see when it's done!
Also, please note that this site is still work in progress. Some things, such as our spell index, aren't here yet. Don't worry, we're giving our best to get it done as soon as possible. Just remember, this is partly a construction site, and certain things might not be perfect as of now. Please do point out mistakes on the existing site, it'll help us all make this into something awesome!

Submit a new Spell

Have you made a great new spell and want it to be in 5eS as well, so everyone can play it? Then feel free to submit it here, and watch it be added to the spell list!
Please use this template for making your own spell. Keep in mind that we may allow ourselves to change any content you submit, or refuse your spell completely. However, we will try to keep your spell's name as you wrote. Feel free to put your spellcaster character's name in it!

Submit a new Project

Do you want us to make something? Do you have a great idea for something new the Many Isles could use? Then feel free to submit the idea, and we might make it true!
Send us your answer below, or mail us at godsofmanyisles@gmail.com!

Become a Partner

If you have a neat RPG product or piece of art that you want to publicize and make available to a great community, you could become a Many Isles partner and get tons of benefits in addition to monthly payment!
However, if you just have a simple homebrew idea/product and want to get feedback on it, you should use our discord or post it on reddit.

We'd love to see what you've got, and will appreciate helping you in your endeavor! Create your partnership in your Adventurer's page, and submit your products there!

Cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (an old sock)

When you cast this spell, target a demon within 2 feet of you. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, you stick the sock on its nose. It must make a Constitution saving throw or fall unconscious for the next 1d4 days.
Higher Levels: You use an additional sock for each level above cantrip you cast this spell at.
