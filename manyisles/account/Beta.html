Beta Gallery

We're constantly creating new stuff, and we try not to throw unrefined goods at users. That's where you can help us! This is a gallery of to-be products (some premium ones, as well), which we entrust upon you to try out and give feedback on. We depend on you, adventurer!
Also feel free to give us feedback and improvement proposals on already published products!

Grand Guide of the Many Isles

Currently work in progress, this guide is destined to become a core piece of the Many Isles, with tons of lore and mechanics. What can you add, correct, or help?

Handbook of Magic Items

The Handbook of Magic Items is a collection of, well, magic items. We're currently expanding it and checking the balance of the various items. Feel free to comment or propose some of your own!

Handbook of Many Herbs

The Handbook of Many Herbs is a reference of plants, mushrooms, herbs that adventurers and their enemies can find in the wilds of Oshmondu. See anything wrong, or want to expand?

Handbook of Mystical Materials

We're working on a collection of materials usable for players and DMs alike. It comes complete with basic crafting rules and two chapters on raw and worked materials. See anything wrong, or want to expand?
