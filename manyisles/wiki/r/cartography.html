Cartography

An essential part of any true fantasy world is what it is. That is represented on maps; thus, we worldbuilders love maps.
This article presents a number of geographical maps as resources for worldbuilders and writers; check out the Political Maps article for further mapping.
Note that all of these maps focus exclusively on Oshmondu, the material plane.

4th Gen Overview

Many Isles 4th generation maps were constituted through a patchwork of detailed regional maps eventually resulting in the complete Overview map in 2018, which is still the face of the Many Isles today. It features island names as well as more precise information.
