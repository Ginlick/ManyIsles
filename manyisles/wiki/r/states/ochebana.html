Ochebana Empire

The Ochebana Empire is a human state that was founded in 620 b. OW, was based in Londerhome, Balebu, and fell in 228 OW.

Literature

Publicly available documents concerning specifically the Ochebana Empire. For larger-scale views, see the Literature article.

Ochebana Empire

Pantheon. Ochebana Empire (A Narrative History). (Many Isles, 2019)
A narrative history of the empire from founding to fall. It does not analyze historical circumstances and reasons, focuses little on general movements and international problems, and is not factual in all respects contradicting any other offical source material.

Technology History

Simon Belt. Technology History. (Private, 2019)
A narrative list of technological innovations within the empire, ordered chronologically. Not all-encompassing, possibly slightly incorrect in certain dates.

Population of Ochebana

A google sheets document with exact population numbers, ordered chronologically, along with two tables to visualize demography. Currently accurate.

Ochebanian Emperors

A complete google sheets document listing Ochebanian emperors, along with all information known about them. Accurate.

Mansions

A short list of all known mansions (noble-run slave farms). Currently accurate, but not a wide assortment of information.
