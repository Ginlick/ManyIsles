Campaigns

A list of publicly available campaigns.

Publicly Available

Under Green Trees

Plays in 524 b. OW in Quirthwood in southern Balebu, within the Ochebana Empire. The characters as adventurers need to protect a village from a fey intrusion, showing weak internal security and the use of adventurers then, as well as a view into Ochebanian rural life.

The Forgotten Islands

Plays in 89 b. OW in the Forgotten Islands, where Denise and Colupan are competing for resources along with a rising ogre threat. Gives an idea of frontier life in that period, as well as tensions between the two human empires.

Walking Dead

Plays in 29 OW in Phandelver, a Dwarven colony in the Mud Hills. Gives a mild insight into dwarven mining operations, as well as the danger of Caverealm intrusions. Somewhat old, wrong names.

Spectre of Revolution

Plays in 225 OW in Intralu, within the Ochebana Empire. Covert nationalists try rallying against imperialists in power, wishing to overthrow imperial rule for popular benefit. Gives insight into the covert struggle as well as conditions at the time, as well as a nice image of Intralian life.

Premium Resources

These can be unlocked with tiers in the digital library.

Gallindor's Guide to Goblinoids

A deep look into goblinoid societies and life.

Almanac of Many Monsters

Although serving primarily as a collection of playable races in the Many Isles, it additionally contains tons of racial lore for most important races.

Common (Language)

Developed with love, a crude indo-germanic language spoken by most inhabitants of Oshmondu. Great for quick immersive speech for writers.

Mildred's Guide to Goblinoids

A look into hags and the lore around them.
