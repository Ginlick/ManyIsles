Many Isles Wiki - Projects

Projects

The Many Isles community is geared towards creation of all kinds, and projects are part of this process. We've got all kinds of running and completed projects where members work on something large that will be publicly available once completed.
For information on running projects, see this spreadsheet.

Got your own projects?

Do you have cool project ideas and are looking for a way to gain access to a large community and wide rage of available tools? Look no further!

The Many Isles try to offer anything a worldbuilder and D&D player might need. We have tons of ongoing projects, and are always open to new suggestions. If you have something specific in mind - say, a tool to create and list homebrew monsters - please get in touch with the Pantheon so we can create something together!
Note that if you want to publish cool homebrews under the OGL, you can immediately start publishing here!
