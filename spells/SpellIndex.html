Slashing Light
Level 0 spell
Evocation
High Magic
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous
Bard, Cleric, Psion, Valkyrie, Wizard

This spell’s damage increases: 5th-2d10, 11th-3d10, 17th-4d10.
