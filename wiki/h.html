Frequently Asked Questions

What's the Many Isles?

We are a homebrew community vowed to the goal of cration. Our tools, such as those presented on this website, serve to help you in this ultimate goal.
By joining the Many Isles, you'll get greater access to these and other tools, and may become part of this great group.

Why a Many Isles account?

Accounts on this website are the core identity of your avatar in the entire community. For any and all purposes, we refer to this account as representative of yourself.
With an account, you can publish products to the digital library, join the community's discord, and start your way up our ranks. Become a leader of the Many Isles!

How to create an account?

To start going, you'll just need to fill out the form, and be on your way! For more details, check out the Accounts article.

How can I publish my homebrew?

You can find the publishing interface in your account page (you need to be signed in and have closed a partnership). For more details, check out the Publishing article.

What's the spell list tool about?

With the spell index, you can look up any 5eS spells, with some created by the community itself!
The spell list allows you to create spell lists adapted to your character's class, race, and piety. Additionally, you can add modules and special spells, and save the whole list! You need to be signed in to save a spell list.
Find more information on this in the Spell List article.
