Many Isles Credit

Many Isles Credit is an easy way to handle transactions within the Many Isles without any transfer fees. Add money to your account and share it across the Many Isles without losing a cent!

Using Credit

Adding Credit

There are multiple easy ways for you to add credit to your account. From your Account Page, you can access a page called "Many Isles Credit". This will redirect you to the purchase page or show your transaction history if you have one.
Alternatively, you can directly access the purchase page via the digital store.
Finally, it is possible that you'll receive credit from other Many Isles users. This can happen if you're a partner and someone supports your work, or if you're a premium publisher.

Using Credit

Many Isles Credit is very useful for any internal money transfers. You can for example easily transfer small amounts of money to publishers you like, supporting their work. You can also pay for Many Isles digital store products in this way.

Further Info

Retrieve the Money

We currently have no automatized way for you to make your credit into real money beyond using it in the Many Isles. If you want to retrieve the money, please directly contact the Pantheon.
