Digital Store Payments

The Many Isles digital store offers a number of paying products, which we primarily use to support our creators.

Our Access to Information

The only personal information we store is account and address details. We never have access to billing credentials, as those are directly supplied on stripe.

Payment Methods

There are two payment methods: stripe and Many Isles Credit.

Stripe

Stripe is an external payment portal which offers money transfers for a small fee. We use it to get money in and out of the Many Isles, and we do not have access to the payment data given to stripe.
The processing fee on transfers is 2.9% + $0.32. This means that we only allow checkouts above $5 so that not too much money is lost in the transfer from the base price.

Many Isles Credit

Credit is our own internal currency, which can be moved between accounts at no cost. This makes it ideal for small payments, such as minor tier purchases and supporting creators. See this article for more information.
