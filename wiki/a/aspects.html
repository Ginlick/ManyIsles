Aspects

In the Many Isles Family System, aspects are services - such as this very website, or the discord server - that are available to users, and administrated locally but under direct Pantheon supervision. For more information on Familial structure, see the Family Structure article.

Structure

The major aspect of Familial structure is the hub, in which multiple aspects representing various platforms are located. For a complete view of the current structure, see the Familial Structure page.
Aspects are administered by a Governor, who holds responsibility towards the Pantheon to handle an aspect responsibly.
An aspect's administration sets that aspect's detailed structure and policies in their Code, however their freedom of choice is greatly restrained by requirements set in the Adventurer's Agreement.
