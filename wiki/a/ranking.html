Ranks in the Many Isles

As you might've noticed while first glancing at your account page, we use a ranking system in the Many Isles to denote an adventurer's status and seniority. Often, titles are granted by special accomplishments or functions.
The information here is taken from §0.3 of the Adventurer's Agreement. We recommend the Legislature and Family System articles for further information.

Tiers

The ranks are grouped in tiers. For information on tier's benefices, see the Tiers article.

Tier 0 (Adventurer)

Generally consisting of freely acquired basic titles, the default title of Adventurer is its lowest part. Its color is blue.

Tier 1

Titles that are tied to more effort, such as investing money into the Many Isles or taking up an executive position, are included in this red tier.

Tier 2 (Hero)

Greater investment is rewarded by the higher of the non-Pantheon tiers.

Tier 3 (Avatar)

Although not true members of the Pantheon, Tier 3 title-holders are greatly invested in the Pantheon, or act as its representatives. The tier is golden.

Tier 4 (God)

Actual Pantheon members are part of the ultimate tier.

Ranks

A complete list of 5eS ranks, as per §0.3.3 - §0.3.7

Adventurer

The Adventurer title is gained as soon as a person creates an account in the Many Isles. An Adventurer can freely access all features of the Many Isles website, gains access to the discord, and is a member of the Many Isles community.

Poet

The Poet title is gained through the editing of publishing of any page on the Many Isles fandom wiki.

Trader

The Trader title is gained through the closing of a companionship with the Many Isles. It grants greater access to the digital library and access to the partnership program as seen in the Trader’s Agreement. This title is lost if the member ends their companionship.

Journeyman

The Journeyman tier 0 title is granted to adventurers performing special services for the Many Isles, such as serving in the administration of an aspect or in an institution.

Dungeon Master

The Dungeon Master tier 1 title is gained by becoming a Dungeon Master of the Many Isles Adventures tool.

Royal Valkyrie

The Royal Valkyrie tier 1 title is gained through the acquisition of the patreon tier with the same name. It grants access to all classes of the Many Isles assortment.

Imperial Soldier

The Imperial Soldier tier 1 title is gained through the acquisition of the patreon tier with the same name. It grants access to all adventures and campaign products of the Many Isles assortment.

Knight

The Knight tier 1 title is granted to special functionaries of Many Isles administration, be it in an aspect or in an institution.

Loremaster

The Grand Wizard tier 2 title is gained through the acquisition of the patreon tier with the same name. It grants access to all lore and similar products of the Many Isles assortment.

Grand Wizard

The Grand Wizard tier 2 title is gained through the acquisition of the patreon tier with the same name. It grants access to rulesets and miscellaneous options of the Many Isles assortment.

High Merchant

The High Merchant tier 2 title is gained through the closing of a premium partnership with the Many Isles. It grants the usual tier 2 benefices, and access to the partnership program as seen in the Trader’s Agreement. This title is lost if the member ends their partnership.

Legendar

The Legendar tier 3 title is gained through the acquisition of the patreon tier with the same name. It grants access to all paid products present on the patreon. It also has special benefits tied to it within the Many Isles.

Master Architect

The Master Architect tier 3 title is gained when a member adds articles, usually lore, to a service of the Many Isles, notably WorldAnvil.

Avatar

The Avatar tier 3 title is gained when a member becomes a moderator on one of the Many Isle’s services. This includes Discord and Reddit. Avatars are nominated by Pantheon representatives.

Younger God

Newer members of the Pantheon, or ones without necessity for absolute power, are granted the title of Younger God. It comes with near-complete control over the Many Isles as well as the huge responsibility of handling the community and keeping it great.

Elder God

The Elder God title is granted to original founders and new masters of the Many Isles, and comes with complete control over the Many Isles as well as the huge responsibility of handling the community and keeping it great.

Roles

Roles are offices in either aspect or institution administration.

Further Information

§0.3.8 Losing a Title

Once a title has been acquired, it is nearly permanent. A title gained by purchase on patreon is not lost if its holder no longer entertains that tier. Exceptions include the High Merchant, which remains only as long as their partnership is upheld. Titles may be removed if a member breaks the Adventurer’s Agreement or through other incorrect actions.
